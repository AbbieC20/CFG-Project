Tips

The best tip we can give is to seek help at the earliest opportunity.
Talk to your friends, your family or partner, but if you want to talk in confidence, talk to your GP.
They can recommend different forms of help, from therapy to medication.
Mental Health issues are not a one size fits all.


Here are a few other things that could help you


    The Worry Tree - download your own copy by clicking the tree
    Try using Worry Time - download more information by clicking the clock
    Get proper sleep - this can be hard, we know
    Exercise - go for a walk, a swim, or a jog if you can
    Drink Water - keeping refreshed is good for you!
