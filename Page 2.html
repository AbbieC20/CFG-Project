Anxiety 101

Anxiety is a normal, if unpleasant, part of life, and it can affect us all in different ways and at different times. Whereas stress is something that will come and go as the external factor causing it (be it a work, relationship or money problems, etc.) comes and goes, anxiety is something that persists whether or not the cause is clear to the sufferer.

Anxiety shows itself in many ways, and there are different types of anxiety. Some people have panic attacks, some people can get irritable, some people can get teary, some have many symptoms but not all of them. If you think you might be suffering from anxiety, we recommend you speak to someone you trust or your GP.
Always remember, you are not alone.
